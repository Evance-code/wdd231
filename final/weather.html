Understanding Weather Reports

METAR

Meteorological Aerodrome Report - current weather conditions at an airport, updated hourly or when significant changes occur.

TAF

Terminal Aerodrome Forecast - weather forecast for an airport, typically covering 24-30 hours, updated every 6 hours.
