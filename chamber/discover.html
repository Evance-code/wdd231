Our Rich History

Tanzania, formed from the union of Tanganyika and Zanzibar in 1964, has a rich cultural heritage spanning centuries. From ancient trade routes to modern economic development, our nation continues to grow while preserving its unique identity.

Home to some of the world's most famous archaeological sites and natural wonders, Tanzania offers both historical significance and modern business opportunities.
